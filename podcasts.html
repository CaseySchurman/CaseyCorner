Radiolab

Radiolab is not only a podcast, but also a radio show on NPR (actually the podcasts are just the radio shows recorded). Hosted by Jad Abumrad and Robert Krulwich, the show focuses on topics of a scientific and philosophical nature. The show attempts to approach broad, difficult topics such as "time" and "morality" in an accessible and light-hearted manner, with a unique audio production style.

I enjoy listening to this podcast (and on the radio if I catch them) because you get to learn about really cool stuff in the world. And their topics cover all sorts of things, from how the earth became what it is today, to telling the story about a unique way a couple is dealing with their young son being diagnosed with terminal cancer. And that's one of the reasons I love this podcast so much, is that it's more of a story. They aren't just sitting behind their microphones telling us facts, but making everything interesting for the listener.
