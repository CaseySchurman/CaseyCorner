Engineering Explained

The Engineering Explained channel's main purpose is to teach people how cars work. And not in a general sense, the channel goes into detail about not only what's going on, but why it's going on, which is really cool. Because then you can actually understand what's happening and why it was made the way it was. Also, the channel explains do's and dont's in certain vehicles, in different situations, etc. Even more impressive is that all the videos are made by one guy, Jason Fenske.

I love this channel because I enjoy learning about cars and how they work, and this channel does by far the best job of explaining concepts so they're easy to understand. So, if you're a car lover, or even just curious about how they work, this channel is the one to go to. It's also a good way of stepping into the world of physics, as Jason does explain some concepts with simple physics and math. See the below video where Jason explains Nitrous.
